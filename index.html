PollutionSight Goals

At PollutionSight, our goal is to keep an eye on pollution with a focus toward the future. We will analyze air pollution data from the United States from the years 2000 to 2016 to identify and visualize patterns and then create a machine learning model to predict future pollutant levels in specific cities.

Is there a correlation between Pollution and Population? The higher the population, the more resources people need. People need more food, water, shelter, and transportation. With there being a demand for resources, it results in more pollution.
